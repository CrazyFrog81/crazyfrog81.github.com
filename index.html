Dr. Wei Zeng is a computer scientist and visualization specialist with research interests on visual analytics, human-computer interaction and deep learning. He joined Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences as an Associate Researcher from March 2018. Before that, he worked as coordinator of the Collaborative Interactive Visualization and Analysis Laboratory (CIVAL) at ETH Zurich’s Future Cities Laboratory in Singapore. He received his B.E. in 2011 and Ph.D. degree in 2015, both from the School of Computer Engineering at Nanyang Technological University. He has over 20 publications at top-tier visualization journals and conferences, including IEEE TVCG, CGF, IEEE VIS, and EuroVis. He won a best paper award from International Conference Information Visualisation.
