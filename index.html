I'm an Associate Professor in Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). I received both the bachelor (2nd Upper Class Honour) and Ph.D. degree in computer sciences from the Nanyang Technological University. My passion on research hinges upon love for visualizations that are simple, trustful, and beautiful. I develop interactive visualizations and visual analytics to improve societal benefits by addressing problems in fields like smart cities and the environment.

Current research interests

  • Computational visualization design: to understand design patterns of visualizations and visual analytics; to develop computational tools to facilitate visualization design.
    • Featured publications: MV layout (TVCG'21), Bayes MV layout (JoV'21), DeepColormap (TVCG'21), VIStory (JoV'21).
    • Techniques can also be applied to floorplan (upcoming) and signboard (upcoming) design.
  • Situated visualization in AR/VR: to develop algorithms for context recognition in AR/VR; to develop new interaction and visualization techniques in AR/VR environment.
    • Featured publications: UrbanVR (C&G'21), FloorLevel-Net (TIP'21), FacadeNet (TIP'21), LassoNet (TVCG'20), Singapore Views (iV'17).
  • Visual analytics: to develop visual analytics for transportation, smart cities, and explainable AI.
    • Featured publications in transportation: Interchange Circos Diagram (CGF'13), MobilityVis (TVCG'14), Waypoints OD (CGF'16), Route-Aware Edge Bundling (CGF'19).
    • Featured publications in smart city: Topology density map (TVCG'21), VitalVizor (CG&A'18), StreetVizor (TVCG'18), MobilityPOI (T-ITS'17).
    • Featured publications in explainable AI: The MAUP problem in traffic prediction (TVCG'21).
I like to work with clever minds from other fields, for example, architects, urban planners, and graphic designers!
